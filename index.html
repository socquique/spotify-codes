Get your Spotify code numbers

Paste a Spotify song, album, playlist or artist link. You'll get the 23 digits to drop into the 3D model so the bars scan to your music.

Get the link in the Spotify app: ••• → Share → Copy link.

In the model (OpenSCAD / MakerWorld Customizer):
  1. Set code_part1 to the digits above.
  2. Leave code_part2 empty and keep digit_max = 7.
  3. Generate — the bars now match your track.
Uses Spotify's public Scannables service. Spotify Codes are scanned in the Spotify app (Search → camera icon). Not affiliated with Spotify.
